FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together and virtual assistance services

What tasks can you help with?

I can assist with almost any administrative task including email management, calendar scheduling, data entry, customer support, research, travel arrangements, document preparation, social media management, CRM updates, and general office support. I'm also happy to discuss custom requirements for your specific business needs.

What are your working hours?

I work standard business hours Monday to Friday. However, I can be flexible for urgent matters and adjust availability based on client time zones. Response time is typically within 2 hours during working hours. For after-hours urgent matters, arrangements can be made in advance.

How do we communicate?

Communication can happen through your preferred channels including email, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, WhatsApp, or phone calls. We will establish regular check-in schedules that work best for you. Daily updates can be provided as needed.

Can I customize a package?

Yes, all packages are fully customizable based on your requirements. I offer hourly rates, part-time, and full-time arrangements. We can start with a trial period to assess your needs before committing to a longer-term arrangement.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out through the contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation call to discuss your specific needs, understand your business, and create a personalized plan. No obligation until you decide to proceed.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, PayPal, Wise, and major credit cards. Invoicing is typically done weekly or monthly depending on our agreement. Payment terms are net-7 for new clients, with flexible arrangements for established ongoing partnerships.

Is my business information secure?

Absolutely. Confidentiality is a core principle of my service. All client information is treated as strictly confidential. I use secure tools and follow best practices for data protection. Non-disclosure agreements can be provided upon request.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients across different time zones globally. I have experience supporting businesses in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. Time zone differences are carefully managed to ensure smooth collaboration.

What if I need to change tasks?

Flexibility is one of the key benefits of working with a virtual assistant. You can change tasks, adjust priorities, and modify requirements at any time. I adapt to your changing business needs without extra charges for routine changes.

Can you handle urgent tasks?

Yes, urgent requests can usually be accommodated. For time-sensitive tasks, please indicate urgency when submitting the request. Emergency outside-hours support can be arranged for existing clients with prior notice.
